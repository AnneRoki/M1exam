MOSCOW

The capitol

Moscow is the capital city of russia since the late 13th century. It is not only the political centre of Russia but also the country's most populous city and its industrial, cultural, scientific and educational capitol.

Below are some popular tourist spots in Moscow

Moscow Kremlin

The Kremlin is a must-see attraction for anyone visiting Moscow.

Home to the nation's top governmental offices, the walled enclosure also houses four cathedrals built in the 15th and 16th century as well as well several notable museums

Saint Basil's Cathedral

Built between 1554 and 1561 and situated in the heart of Moscow.

St.Basil's Cathedral has been among the top tourist attractions in Russia.

Gorky Central Park of Culture and Leisure

Gorky Central Park of Culture and Leisure is a central park in Moscow, named after Maxim Gorky.

Gorky Park is the most famous park of Moscow, created during the Soviet period as a hub for relaxation and cultural activities.

The State Tretyakov Gallery

The State Tretyakov Gallery is Moscow art museum founded Pavel M. Tretyakov in 1856.

It contains the world's finest collection of 17th and 18th century Russian icons having more than 40,000 of them. There are also 18th-century portraits, 19th-century historical paintings, and works of the Soviet period.
